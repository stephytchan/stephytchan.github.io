Stephanie Chan

Department of Mathematics, University College London, Gower Street, London, WC1E 6BT, United Kingdom
Email: stephanie.chan[at]ucl.ac.uk

I am a lecturer in pure mathematics at UCL.

My research area is number theory, in particular arithmetic statistics.

Previous affiliations

I was
a postdoc in the Browning Group at the Institute of Science and Technology Austria (June 2023 to August 2025);
a postdoctoral fellow in the Diophantine Geometry program at SLMath (MSRI) (January to May 2023);
a postdoctoral assistant professor at the University of Michigan (August 2020 and December 2022).
I completed my PhD at UCL in August 2020 under the supervision of Andrew Granville.
